About Us

Convenience

We're accepting online rental payment to save tenants' time, besides that we also provide online portal for tenant to report any issue of the hostel to us 24/7. Postal receiving service also provide to our tenant to bring convenience to them on receiving parcel or letters.

Comfortable

With the facility provided in the hostel rooms and public area, we are able to deliver a cozy environment for our tenants to stay and study.

Good Service

We always like to hear our tenant' feedback and complaints. Their questions and needs are always answered within 24 hours and resolved fast and possible by the management.

Introducing Uveve Management

Uveve Management has been started hostel accommodation management since year 2015 and till today, we've been serving our tenants here for 2 years and counting. We are one of the leading hostel accommodation management in Malaysia.

Our mission is to provide comfort, safe, clean convenient and efficient services to all of our tenants by creating and maintaining a comfortable environment in its accommodation in order to match with our company slogan "Your Comfortability, Our First Priority". As for landlords, our mission is to secure your time and cost, convenient and increase your property value.
