Is there any contract for renting a room?

Yes, there is 1 year contract upon renting any room under Uveve hostel management.

What if I cancel my contract before it ends?

Your deposit will not be refunded or transferred to any other services.

What kind of facilities are provided in my room?

Most of our rooms is provided with 1 bed with mattress, 1 study table and 1 wardrobe.
You can find the details of furniture provided in your room by referring to the following links:

What is the internet speed provided for the hostel?

Most of our hostels are equip with Streamyx 4mbps and some with Streamyx 8mbps internet. However, to avoid heavy usage of 1 user bring down the whole network, we've implement Quality Of Service (QoS) to control each tenant connection should reach a maximum of 1Mbps to maintain the fair user policy usage for all the other tenant in the house.

Does the rental included the utility bills?

We've separated electricity meter and water meter every single floor. The electricity and water bills will be divided among the tenants staying on that particular floor. Tenants can view or pay their utility bill via Tenant Portal

How can I do my rental, electricity and water bill payment?

You can approach to our office to do your bill payment of your rental, electricity and water bill. We also accept online transfer to pay your bills for your convenience. More details can be found on our Tenant Portal.

Is smoking and keep pets allowed in the hostel / common area?

Sorry, these action are not allowed in the hostel

Is that cooking is allowed in the hostel area?

Cooking is discouraged in the hostel. However, simple cooking like instant noodle, instant porridge or using rice cooker is acceptable. However, tenants must clean up the area after cooking.

What kind of security measurements is provided?

24 hours gated and guarded housing area with security guards patrol every 2 hours. Smart card are required to enter all Uveve Management houses to ensure the tenant's security.

How to register my computer or smart phone MAC address to my hostel network?

Please refer to the following links based on the device or operating system you are using:

Windows Vista, 7, 8, 8.1 or 10     iPhone / iPad     Andriod Phone / Tablet     Windows Phone 7 Or Above     Windows XP     Mac OS X 10.5 Or Above     Mac OS X 10.4 Or Older     Linux OS or Ubuntu

After recorded down your Mac address, please Login your tenant account and go to "Profile" and click on "Update" link on Mac address 1 and Mac address 2. Your update will take up to 24 hours to be updated to your hostel network.

There is leakage, spoil of items in the hostel. What should I do?

You can submit a Service Request in your tenant account by Login Here or report your issue to our office during office hour by calling +605-1234567.

I have lost my room key or smart card, what should I do?

Please approach Uveve Management office immediately to report your key or smart card is lost. To concern your security, we will replace the your room's door lock and a fine shall levied to the tenant.

**Please take note that management team will not be responsible on any item lost in tenant's room if tenant are not reporting their lost of key immediately.
